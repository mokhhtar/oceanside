Save Your Hair from the Coast

Living by the ocean is wonderful for the soul but tough on the hair. Saltwater is hygroscopic, meaning it draws moisture out of your hair, leaving it brittle and prone to breakage. Combined with the strong UV rays in Oceanside, this leads to oxidation and dryness. Our treatment menu is designed specifically to combat these local environmental stressors.

Our Treatment Menu

  • Keratin Smoothing Treatment: Fight the marine layer frizz! This treatment infuses keratin protein into the hair cuticle, smoothing it down and blocking out humidity. It reduces styling time by 50% and lasts up to 4 months.
  • Olaplex Bonding Service: Essential for bleached or highlighted hair. This scientific breakthrough rebuilds the broken disulfide bonds in your hair, making it stronger and healthier from the inside out.
  • Deep Hydration Masque: A quick but powerful injection of moisture using steam therapy to penetrate the hair shaft. Perfect as an add-on to any cut.
  • Scalp Detox: Healthy hair starts at the root. We remove buildup from sand, salt, and products to unclog follicles and promote healthy growth.

The "Oceanside Finish" Blowout

No haircut is complete without a professional finish. Our signature blowout includes the use of humidity-resistant products that seal the cuticle and provide shine that lasts for days. Whether you want volume, stick-straight sleekness, or bouncy curls, you'll leave our salon feeling runway-ready.
